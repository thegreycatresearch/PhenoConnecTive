# Phenotypes

## Joint Hypermobility
- common in multiple EDS subtypes
- associated with instability and subluxations

---

## Skin Hyperextensibility
- characteristic of classical EDS
- often associated with soft skin

---

## Vascular Fragility
- strongly associated with vascular EDS
- increased arterial rupture risk

---

## Chronic Pain
- multisystemic symptom
- common in connective tissue disorders
